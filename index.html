More about SISTec

Sagar Institute of Science and Technology (SISTEC), Bhopal was established in the year 2007. The college is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal. SISTEC Bhopal offers B.Tech, MBA and M.Tech programmes. The courses are offered by Sagar Institute of Science and Technology in different specialisations.
